OUR HISTORY

Judit Cercós is a Catalan sommelier who worked at the Mandarin in Barcelona while Ludovic Dubois exercised his talents as a chef at Rostang or at Jacques Cagna. It is in the vibrant Catalan city that they met while they were working there.

Having become a couple in life, they moved to Paris and decided to combine their know-how by opening Les Poulettes Batignolles in 2014, a restaurant that quickly attracted epicureans and gourmets from the area. In 2018, they took over the premises adjoining their restaurant. The idea was to offer a different experience, but complementary to what they already offered to Les Poulettes.

This is how Poussin was born, a gourmet, epicurean and joyful dining cellar, which reflects the mix of French and Iberian gastronomic cultures.

OUR CORE TEAM

Ludovic is a creative chef who makes you explore his new creations and assemblages of subtle flavors and textures. Its cuisine adapts to today's market arrivals, from suppliers with impeccable know-how and work ethic, working with high quality products.
Judit is a seasoned and passionate sommelier who has delighted herself by concocting a nice selection of nearly 130 “skittles” of natural or biodynamic wines from France, Spain or Italy that are sure to surprise you.
Morgan, Judit and Ludo's accomplice, greets you behind the counter and is there to advise you and make sure you don't go missing.

CONCEPT TITLE

Poussin and Poulettes being two adjoining brands belonging to the same house, they of course share common characteristics: they both offer market cuisine, based on quality products, with a strong creative touch, often land / sea combinations, and strong French and Iberian influences.

When it comes to wines, the 2 establishments are not left out: they benefit from the expertise of Judit's sommelier, who has selected wines that will leave no one unmoved!

If the raw products are sourced from the same suppliers and processed by the same teams, the concepts are different:

Poulettes is a traditional restaurant / bistro, while Poussin is a dining cellar that invites sensory, festive and convivial exploration. The plates to share are well matched with a little wine from behind the bundles. All can be enjoyed in a good mood and in a tasteful musical atmosphere on the light Japanese-style wooden counter or on one of the large high tables that adorn the small room.

Obviously, because of this proximity, it is not uncommon for customers comfortably installed at Poussin's to order a dish from Poulettes ... Likewise, it is common for customers from Poulettes to go for an aperitif or a digestif at Poussin. … There is total synergy between the two entities.
